All of Me is a jazz standard by Gerald Marks & Seymour Simons written in 1931. It was first performed by Belle Baker over the radio and the first recording was made in December 1931 by Ruth Etting. It has become a standard in the jazz musicians repertoire and countless recordings and arrangements have been created.
